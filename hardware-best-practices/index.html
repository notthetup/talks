Semantic Versioning - SemVer

  • Pseudo-standard 🤷‍♂️
  • Widely adopted 🙌
  • MAJOR.MINOR.PATCH
"we propose a simple set of rules and requirements that dictate how version numbers are assigned and incremented"

Our SemVer

  • MAJOR: Changes that affect other components 🙈
  • MINOR: Changes that only affect your component 💪
  • PATCH: Changes that only affect your component but don't change any functionality 🧹
"understand what changed at a glance"
